About the Event

Welcome to the Art Trade Fair, a place where comic design enthusiasts, nerds, creatives, and fans of fictional heroes can come together. This event is all about sharing, selling, and exchanging art, whether it's traditional or digital.

Event Details

  • Date: June 28th
  • Time: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Location: Theo Thijssen-gebouw, 3rd Floor, HvA
  • Activities: Art booths, live demos, art exchanges, and more!

Event Goals

This fair aims to build a community for comic design enthusiasts. Share your passion for graphic design, gain inspiration, interact with like-minded individuals, and get recognition for your work.

Workshops

Join our exclusive workshop on learning how to sew and complete your own costumes. Perfect for cosplay enthusiasts!

Conditions & Do's / Don'ts

  • Respect other participants and their artwork.
  • No selling of counterfeit or unauthorized merchandise.
  • Keep your booth area clean and tidy.
  • Follow instructions from event organizers.
  • Have fun and enjoy the event!
